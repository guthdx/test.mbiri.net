People who have healthy hearts may do better if they get COVID-19. This study looked at heart health scores from thousands of people using a tool called "Life's Essential 8." Key Takeaways

People with the highest cardiovascular health scores on the American Heart Association's Life's Essential 8 metric were 41% less likely to experience severe COVID-19 outcomes, including hospitalization and death. This study demonstrates the critical connection between baseline cardiovascular health and pandemic resilience.

Did You Know? The Life's Essential 8 includes diet quality, physical activity, nicotine exposure, sleep health, body mass index, cholesterol levels, blood glucose, and blood pressure. Maintaining healthy levels across these metrics may protect against severe illness from infectious diseases like COVID-19.
