About This Tool

This worksheet is based on the American Heart Association's Life's Essential 8, a set of key health behaviors and clinical measures that have been shown to impact heart health.

It's not a test... it's a tool. You just select the answer that fits you best in each section. At the end, you'll add up your points to get a total score.

ü©∫ Blood Pressure

Blood pressure is measured with two numbers (like 120/80). The first number (systolic) measures pressure when your heart beats. The second (diastolic) measures pressure when your heart rests between beats.

Where to get it checked: Doctor's office, pharmacy, health fair, or home monitor.

ü©∏ Blood Sugar (A1C)

A1C is a blood test that shows your average blood sugar level over the past 2-3 months. It's measured as a percentage.

Where to get it tested: Doctor's office, clinic, or community health screening.

üß™ Cholesterol (non-HDL)

Non-HDL cholesterol is calculated from your cholesterol panel blood test. It's your total cholesterol minus your "good" (HDL) cholesterol.

Where to get it tested: Doctor's office, clinic, or community health screening.
